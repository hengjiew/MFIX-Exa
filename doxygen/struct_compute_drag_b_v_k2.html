Returns: the calculated drag coefficient.

Inputs: EPg - gas volume fraction Mug - gas laminar viscosity ROpg - gas density * EP_g vrel - magnitude of gas-solids relative velocity DPM - particle diameter of solids phase M DPA - average particle diameter PHIS - solids volume fraction of solids phases fvelx - x component of the fluid velocity at the particle position fvely - y component of the fluid velocity at the particle position fvelz - z component of the fluid velocity at the particle position i, j, k - particle cell indices pid - particle id number
